Professional Trade Solutions

Advanced Surface Innovations transforms every windscreen and glass service into a strategic revenue driver for truck dealerships, bus operators, mechanics and fleet managers across Melbourne. By combining precision stone-chip repairs, multi-stage scratch remediation and full ADR-compliant glass replacement, each backed by batch-certified traceability, you can offer premium add-on services that customers value and willingly pay for, all while boosting resale appeal.

As Australia's exclusive distributor of all APEAX Films, we integrate high-performance innovative protective films & coatings that elevate vehicle presentation, safeguard surfaces and extend asset lifespan. With rapid turnaround, consistent OEM-grade finish and rigorous ISO documentation, ASI empowers your workshop & dealerships to capture premium margins, strengthen customer loyalty and stand out in a competitive wholesale and trade market.

Our Wholesale & Trade Services

🔧

Precision Glass Remediation Services

Crack & Chip Repair: Chips and cracks repaired via resin injection to rebuild structural integrity and prevent spread.

Thermal-stress Fracture Stabilisation: UV-cure systems for rapid cure and return to service.

Wiper-Arc & Etch Remediation: Precision micro-abrasion to remove wiper-arc "rainbow" marks without annealing the OEM seal.

Chemical-etch Removal: Light graffiti removal preserving factory coatings and acoustic dampening layers.

🔄

OEM-Grade Windscreen & Side-Glass Replacement

Full Windscreen & Side-Glass Change-out: Using original-equipment specification glazing, including acoustic calibration.

Silicone Window Seal Damage Remediation: Professional seal repair and replacement services.

Micro-Scratch & Surface Restoration: Precision multi-step nano-abrasive polishing process removing micro-fissures, swirl marks and surface hazing.

Multi-stage Buffing: Dedicated clearing compound to refine finish and restore full optical clarity.

🛡️

HydroGuard™ Surface Protection Coating

Hydrophobic Performance: Self-cleaning surface that sheds water, road grime and brake dust, reducing wiper drag by up to 40%.

UV-Resistant Shield: Blocks >98% of UV radiation preventing glass micro-etching and seal-degradation.

Abrasion & Chemical Defence: Hard ceramic-infused polymers guard against scratches, acid rain etch and environmental fallout.

Fleet Longevity: Up to 60% reduction in full-glass replacements and 25% drop in unscheduled downtime.

Add-On Revenue: Turnkey premium service that enhances your portfolio and boosts per-vehicle revenue.

💡

Optical Surface Restoration (Headlights & Lenses)

ISO-compliant Headlight Restoration: Sequential UV damage removal with refined ASI processes, polishing and GClear™ Clear Coat application.

3-Year Protection: Eliminates yellowing/clouding and protects from UV damage for 3 years.

Polymer-lens Rejuvenation: For taillights, spotlights and indicators, removing oxidation and restoring optical clarity to OEM levels.
