Product Overview

Apeax Films' Xtreme OptiShield is a premium Window Protection Shield (WPS) designed to deliver advanced defense for glass surfaces across heavy automotive, commercial, residential and marine applications. Engineered for maximum durability without compromising clarity, Xtreme OptiShield provides powerful protection against chips, cracks, scratches, and everyday impact, all while maintaining flawless, crystal-clear visibility. It's the ultimate choice for those who expect superior performance, long-lasting protection, and a sleek, invisible finish.

Industry Applications

Bus & Coach Fleets

Use Case: Public and charter bus operations face frequent windscreen wear from high daily kilometers, variable weather, and environmental abrasion (e.g., wiper arcs, sandblasting).

OptiShield Benefits:

  • Shields ADAS-calibrated windscreens against premature degradation.
  • Reduces unscheduled downtime due to glass damage.
  • Ensures optical clarity per ADR and AS/NZS standards.
  • Cuts replacement costs and supports up to 60% lifecycle savings when paired with ASI remediation services.

Heavy Vehicles

Use Case: Trucks and utility fleets operating in mining, construction, and logistics environments endure persistent debris impacts, UV exposure, and chemical splash risks.

OptiShield Benefits:

  • Offers impact resistance from gravel, mud, and chemical splash zones.
  • Enhances driver visibility and safety under harsh glare or abrasion conditions.
  • Minimizes windscreen replacement frequency, reducing vehicle off-road time.
  • Fleet-wide film traceability and service compliance ensures QA accountability.

Trains & Trams

Use Case: Rail networks experience long-term surface wear, UV-related haze, and graffiti incidents on forward-facing glass and driver visibility panels.

OptiShield Benefits:

  • Defends against urban pollutants, acid rain, and physical surface wear.
  • Preserves visibility and reduces optical distortion, critical for operator safety.
  • Compliant with fixed asset maintenance cycles and visibility standards.
  • Supports long-term surface integrity on older fleet glass without full replacement.

Marine Vessels

Use Case: Ferries, pilot boats, and patrol craft are exposed to saline environments, UV degradation, and chemical cleaning cycles that corrode standard glazing.

OptiShield Benefits:

  • Provides a hydrophobic and UV-resistant layer ideal for marine glare reduction.
  • Prevents salt corrosion and microetching of bridge glass.
  • Reduces maintenance frequency and preserves navigational clarity.
  • Enables long-term protection of specialty optical surfaces like radar domes and wheelhouse screens.
