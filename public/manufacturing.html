Industrial-Grade Manufacturing Solutions

ASI delivers precision-engineered glass solutions for Australia's manufacturing sector. Our products are designed to withstand the demanding conditions of industrial environments while maintaining optical clarity and structural integrity.

From safety glazing for machinery to specialized viewing panels for production equipment, we understand the critical role that quality glass plays in manufacturing operations and worker safety.

Our Manufacturing Services

⚡

Deep Scratch Remediation

Production-Grade Extraction: Targeted removal of deep substrate scratches and gouges using controlled multi-stage polishing sequences, restoring glass to OEM clarity per service workflow.

Batch-Level Traceability: Complete logging of polishing compounds, pad specifications and process cycles, aligned to your ADR and ISO audit requirements.

Integrated Compliance: Dust suppression, wastewater capture and operator PPE validations delivered just-in-time to your maintenance facility.

⚙️

Technical Integration Support

CAD-to-CAD Data Exchange: Seamless integration for hole-patterns, sensor mounts and bonded interfaces.

On-Site Engineering Liaison: Support during prototype trials, first-off builds and design iterations.

Collaborative Development: End-to-end technical partnership for optimal manufacturing outcomes.

💧

HydroGuard Surface Protection Coatings

High-Performance Protection: Hydrophobic and UV-resistant coating engineered to reduce abrasion, corrosion and chemical attack.

Extended Service Life: Increases service life by up to 60% and maintains optical clarity in high-wear environments.

Flexible Application: Can be applied pre-assembly or retrofit-applied to in-process panels, with full QA sign-off.

Aftermarket Repair & Rework Services

Rapid On-Site Repair: Quick crack and chip repair systems to minimise line stoppages or fleet-entry delays.

Controlled Rework: Haze removal, edge refinishing and adhesive touch-ups for off-spec or returned parts.

Preventative Audits: Glass health audits to catch stress fractures before they propagate.

DEEP SCRATCH REMEDIATION

At ASI, we specialise in precision glass scratch remediation for vehicles, buses, marine vessels and architectural installations. Our expert technicians employ industry-leading techniques and ISO-aligned processes to eradicate surface blemishes without distortion. From windscreen repairs to large-format glazing to manufacturing line glass, we restore optical clarity, enhance safety and extend asset life, delivering flawless, showroom-quality results every time.

OVERSPRAY REMOVAL

Within our ISO-aligned manufacturing sector, Advanced Surface Innovations employs a precision, multi-stage polishing regimen to strip paint overspray, blasting residue and micro-contaminants from glass assemblies without introducing distortion. Every batch of polishing compounds, pad types and cycle settings is recorded for full ADR and ISO traceability, while integrated dust-extraction, wastewater-capture and operator-safety controls ensure zero production-line interruptions. The result is showroom-quality glass, be it bonded windscreens, large-format panels or transit-grade assemblies, with flawless optical clarity, enhanced durability and complete compliance delivered just-in-time for your build sequence.
