Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30/09/2025

Introduction

ASI respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information across our website, applications, social media accounts, and digital services such as LinkedIn and ASI Portal.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our Services:

  • Personal Information you provide (such as name, email, phone number, or LinkedIn profile data if you connect via LinkedIn).
  • Usage Information including IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and engagement with our content.
  • Social Media Data when you interact with us via LinkedIn, Instagram, or other platforms, in line with the privacy settings of those platforms.
  • Content Submissions such as comments, messages, or uploads you share through our website or social media accounts.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our Services.
  • Personalize your experience across our website, applications, and integrations.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Share updates, marketing, or service announcements (with opt-out options).
  • Ensure security, compliance, and fraud prevention.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. LinkedIn & Third-Party Integrations

If you use our LinkedIn integration or other social logins, we may access profile information and activity only as permitted by you and the platform. Data is used solely for providing the integration’s intended functionality and is not shared externally.

Each third-party platform (e.g., LinkedIn, Instagram, GitHub) maintains its own privacy rules. Please review their policies to understand how they handle your information.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored securely with appropriate safeguards.
  • Access is limited to authorized personnel.
  • We apply industry-standard measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other laws, including:

  • Accessing your data.
  • Requesting correction or deletion.
  • Withdrawing consent (such as revoking LinkedIn integration).
  • Opting out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: sales@asi-australia.com.au

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand usage, improve performance, and deliver relevant content. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Content & Works

Any content, media, or works we publish (e.g., blogs, product documentation, videos, or posts) remain our property. If you engage with or share this content, your interactions (likes, comments, reposts) may be publicly visible depending on your platform settings.

8. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at asi-australia.com.au/privacy-policy with the effective date shown at the top.

9. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: sales@asi-australia.com.au
  • Website: asi-australia.com.au
