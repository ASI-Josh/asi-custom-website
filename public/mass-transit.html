Mass-Transit & Fleets

Premium glass solutions for public transportation, commercial fleets, and specialized vehicles

Industry-Leading Transit Solutions

Hyperglass Innovations is Australia's emerging specialist in automotive glass, life-extension, performance enhancement and surface remediation, designed to enhance maintenance and pre-delivery inspection services for bus, train and heavy-vehicle fleets. We continually evaluate and integrate innovative solutions into our portfolio, from restoring deep-scratches to OEM clarity, applying HydroGuard, a nanotechnology-based coating that repels water, dirt and abrasions plus the distribution & fitting of all APEAX™ XTREME films to protect & enhance automotive glass & body life, and keep costs at a minimum, without causing extreme downtime.

By embedding these proven technologies into your fleet maintenance programmes, you'll cut replacement costs, extend service intervals, maximise asset uptime and keep every windscreen crystal-clear with minimal overhead.

Glass Remediation Services

Wiper Arc Repair: Precision polishing to eliminate wear patterns and restore optical clarity.

Chip & Bullseye Repair: Fast-fill technology to stabilize and restore minor damage.

Side Glass Scratch Correction: Deep scratch removal from mechanical or environmental abrasion.

Graffiti Removal: Eliminates chemical etching and paint without damaging OEM glass.

📋

Fleet Site Inspections & Regional Deployment

Depot-Driven Inspections: Technician-led site inspections tailored to your fleet, depot layout, and operational rhythm.

We Assess: Windscreens, side glass, polymer lenses, damage types, on-site logistics, and scheduling alignment.

HyperPortal Integration: Vehicle condition profiles, depot dashboards, and traceable QA records.

Coverage: Melbourne Metro & Regional Victoria - from Geelong to Bendigo, Ballarat, Shepparton, and surrounds.

HydroGuard Glass Protection

Hydrophobic Nano-Coating: Long-term protection against acid rain, UV degradation, and chemical attack.

Wiper Drag Reduction: Extends wiper and glass life while improving driver visibility.

Environmental Impact Minimisation

Glass Life Extension: Restoring instead of replacing diverts significant glass volumes from landfill.

ISO 14001-Aligned: All products and procedures comply with environmental management standards.

Water-Conscious & Chemical-Free: Optimized processes with low-flow techniques and low-toxicity agents.
