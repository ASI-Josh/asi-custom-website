Glass Remediation & Life-Extension Solutions

All ASI Technicians follow an Advanced, multi-stage nano-abrasive polishing and clearing process that remediates deep-scratches, micro-fissures, swirl marks and hazing to restore OEM-grade optical clarity.

  • Chip & Crack Repairs: Precision resin injection to stabilise damage, prevent spread & minimize light refraction.
  • Deep Scratch & Swirl-Mark Removal: Multi-stage nano-abrasive polishing to recover factory-clear finish without affecting optical clarity.
  • Glass Surface Decontamination & Polish: Removes buildup of deposits, mineral haze, chemical etch and more for a pristine substrate.
  • Scratch Graffiti & Acid-Pen Graffiti Remediation: Precision nano-abrasive polishing to lift and erase graffiti etchings, restoring a smooth, clear glass surface without weakening integrity.
  • Hydroguard Protection Coating: A nano-ceramic sealant that bonds invisibly to glass, repelling water, dirt and UV damage while boosting abrasion resistance and simplifying cleaning.

  • Mobile & Workshop Service: Flexible on-site or workshop-based replacements to suit urban and regional fleet logistics.

Polymer UV Damage Remediation

Specialized restoration techniques for UV-damaged polymer roof strips, restoring color and protection to extend service life.

  • UV Damage Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation of polymer degradation and color fading to determine optimal restoration approach.
  • Surface Preparation: Thorough cleaning and conditioning to ensure maximum adhesion and effectiveness of restoration treatments.
  • Color Restoration: Specialized compounds and techniques to restore original color depth and vibrancy to faded polymer surfaces.
  • UV Protection Coating: Application of advanced UV-resistant sealants to prevent future degradation and extend service life.
  • Quality Assurance: Post-treatment inspection and documentation to ensure restoration meets performance standards.

APEAX Xtreme™ Series

Our comprehensive range of premium protection films designed for automotive, marine, and commercial applications:

Advanced headlight restoration and protection for automotive lighting. A controlled multi-stage nano-abrasive correction removes oxidation, haze and micro-scratches, then a durable clear-coat is applied for OEM-grade optical clarity. The coating improves UV stability, minimises future hazing and adds hydrophobic performance for easier cleaning, helping maintain light output and appearance for up to 3 years when properly maintained.

High-performance windscreen protection film made from optical-grade TPU with ultra-clear visibility and a hard-coated surface. Helps resist stone-chip pitting and chemical etchants while blocking >99% UV. Designed for bus/coach, heavy-duty transport, rail, marine craft and architectural glazing where impact resistance and clarity matter. Not ADR-approved for standard passenger windscreen.

High-performance window tint available in two grades—Carbon and Ceramic—each delivering >99% UV block and substantial heat rejection:

Carbon Grade: Entry-level film offering Total Solar Energy Rejection (TSER) >50% at 30% Visible Light Transmission (VLT). Cost-effective protection against heat and glare while preserving exterior aesthetics.

Ceramic Grade: Premium multilayer, sputtered film achieving TSER >65% at 35% VLT. Maximises glare reduction, cabin comfort and interior fade prevention with minimal colour shift.

Ideal for automotive, marine and commercial glazing applications down under, with both grades engineered to maintain clarity and durability in harsh UV environments.

Removable anti-graffiti / anti-scratch film with a hard-coated, ultra-smooth face that resists spray paints, inks, adhesives and light glass-etching attempts. When tagged, simply peel the sacrificial top layer to reveal clean glass beneath, reducing cleaning labour, avoiding glass replacement and minimising downtime in high-risk transit environments.

Premium self-healing paint protection film for high-mileage transit exteriors, available in Gloss and Matte (Satin) finishes. Made from durable aliphatic TPU with a micro-encapsulated topcoat that re-levels fine abrasions and swirl marks under ambient heat or warm water, preserving either a deep gloss or uniform matte look. Provides added defence against chips, scratches, UV, road chemicals and staining, with anti-yellowing performance. Maintains factory colour and surface texture while reducing rework and downtime.
