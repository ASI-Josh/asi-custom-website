Marine Glass & Surface Protection Solutions

Keep your vessel's glass and exterior surfaces pristine and protected with ASI's specialist marine remediation, coating & film services. Our Marine Glass Decontamination service eradicates salt, mineral encrustation and biofilm to restore perfect clarity. Our Precision Scratch Remediation polishes away blemishes for uninterrupted visibility through windshields and windows. Our HydroGuard™ Marine Coating delivers a durable, water-repellent barrier that resists salt spray, UV damage and staining, cutting maintenance downtime by up to 60 percent.

For comprehensive exterior & interior protection, we supply and install APEAX XTREME films tailored for marine applications: OptiShield™ delivers premium windscreen and glass protection; PaintShield™ guards painted hulls and superstructures, RadShield™ offers high-performance window tinting for glare reduction and UV defense, and GrafShield™ provides an interior anti-scratch barrier for cabin glass. Each film ensures an OEM-grade finish, corrosion resistance and extended asset lifespan, all backed by full ISO-aligned traceability and Australian-approved materials.
